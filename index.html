Classroom Mode Rules (Prototype)

  • 5-card opening hand, one manual draw per turn.
  • +1 max Inspiration each turn (cap 5), refreshes at turn start.
  • Play cards as long as Inspiration allows. Matching your active Author lowers character cost by 1.
  • Characters can attack once per turn after they are played.
  • Literary Device cards and quick checks award Knowledge points on correct answers.
  • Win by reducing opposing Reputation to 0 or reaching the Teacher Mode Knowledge target.
